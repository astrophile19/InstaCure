# InstaCure
 This is an holistic app where booking doctor appointments are one click away from  wherever you are.Now you could consult doctor being at your home and do not have to wait for long list of patients to get your turn.


#Modules
1)Admin
  #Features
   ->Update personal info.
   ->Grant Appointments.
   ->Assign Staff.
   ->Manage Bills.
   ->Manage Users.
   ->View Testimonials.
2)Doctor
    #Features
    ->Update personal info.
    ->Add Specialization.
    ->Set Slot
    ->Upload Patient Report
    ->View Staff Assigned
    ->Provide Feedback

3)Patient
#Features
    ->Update personal info.
    ->Book Appointments.
    ->View Reports
    ->Manage Bills	
    ->Provide Feedback
4)Staff
#Features
    ->Update personal info.
    ->Report to Doctor
    ->Provide Feedback
